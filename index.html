International Conference on
Computer Science and Emerging Technologies 

(CSET-2024)

The International Conference on Computer Science and Emerging Technologies will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and research results in theory, methodology and applications of Computer Science and its allied areas. The conference looks for significant contributions to all major fields of the Computer Science such as Hardware, Computer System organization, Networks, Software and its Engineering, Theory of Computation, Mathematics of computing, Information Systems, Security and Privacy, Human Centered Computing, Computing methodologies, Applied computing, Technology Management, Smart cities etc.
